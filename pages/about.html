Who We Are

Diligence & Intelligence (D&I) is a global data services partner that specializes in providing comprehensive data solutions to businesses. We are a data-focused technology startup driven by the mission to empower businesses with smarter, faster, and more actionable insights. Founded by professionals with extensive experience across diverse industries — from retail and e-commerce to finance and telecommunications — we understand that no two data journeys are the same.

Whether you're a large enterprise or a scaling business, we help you unlock the full value of your data.

Our team team of experts specializes in delivering both cloud-based and on-premises data solutions, tailored to your infrastructure, security needs, and business goals. We design, build, and support everything from centralized data platforms and modern data pipelines to robust reporting and BI systems.

We have a proven edge in working with retail companies, especially those operating in omni-channel environments (offline stores, online shops, mobile apps, and social platforms). If you're a business looking to digitally transform, centralize your data, or start your analytics journey, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Mission

At D&I, our mission is to empower businesses with the data-driven insights they need to thrive in a competitive landscape. We leverage advanced analytics and innovative technologies to transform raw data into valuable information that informs strategic decisions.
