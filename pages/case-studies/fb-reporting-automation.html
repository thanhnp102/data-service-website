Automating F&B Reporting with Power BI

Transforming manual Excel processes into automated insights for a leading wine company

Duration

3 months

Team Size

3 Power BI Developers

Impact

97% Time Saved

The Challenge

The client faced significant operational inefficiencies:

  • Manual updating of 200+ large Excel files monthly
  • Over 100 hours spent on manual data consolidation
  • Managing 1,000+ SKUs across 10+ regions
  • Delayed reporting affecting decision-making
  • High risk of manual errors in calculations

Our Solution

We implemented a comprehensive Power BI transformation:

  • Mapped and integrated 100+ essential KPIs
  • Designed seamless migration from Excel to Power BI
  • Automated data integration and refresh processes
  • Created dynamic, interactive dashboards
  • Provided comprehensive team training

Impact & Results

97%

Reduction in manual effort (100+ hrs to 2-3 hrs)

24x

More frequent updates (monthly to hourly)

99%

Reporting accuracy achieved

200+

Excel files automated

The automation transformation delivered dramatic efficiency improvements, virtually eliminating manual reporting efforts. The shift from monthly to daily/hourly updates enabled more agile business decisions, while automated calculations and scheduled refreshes achieved near-perfect reporting accuracy.
