Pharmaceutical Data Migration & Analytics Overhaul

Transforming data analytics for a leading multinational pharmaceutical company

Duration

10 months

Team Size

5 Developers

Impact

30% Cost Reduction

The Challenge

The client faced multiple challenges in managing their extensive data infrastructure:

  • Complex data scattered across multiple platforms (SAP HANA, PostgreSQL, BigQuery)
  • Over 10 data sources and 100 tables to manage
  • 500+ Tableau dashboards proving costly and inflexible
  • Data silos hindering comprehensive analysis
  • Need for strict security between pharmacy partners

Our Solution

We implemented a comprehensive migration and restructuring strategy:

  • Migrated 200+ critical dashboards to Power BI and Looker
  • Designed optimized data schemas and modeling techniques
  • Implemented cold storage solutions for less accessed data
  • Established a centralized analytics platform for 500+ pharmacies
  • Implemented strict data security protocols
  • Conducted targeted training sessions for internal teams

Impact & Results

30%

Reduction in overall analytics costs

50%

Reduction in query costs

2x

Faster reporting speed

20+

Team members trained

The strategic overhaul delivered substantial benefits with near real-time dashboards providing insights into logistics, commercial performance, and clinical trials. The centralized platform effectively broke down data silos, while comprehensive training empowered the internal team to independently manage their analytics workflows.
